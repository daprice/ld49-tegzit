Content warning

This is a satirical game about the Texas government's handling of the 2021 winter storm, a recent event in which people died.

I've lived in Texas my whole life and wrote this game as an attempt to find a humorous way to express frustration with the Texas government's callous response to the event, and to poke fun at the conflicts of interest of many of the politicians responsible. In this game, you play as a satirical version of one of those politicians.

If you aren't comfortable with any of this for any reason, there's no shame in not playing, or stopping if it makes you uncomfortable. Instead, if you live in Texas, I'd ask that you consider registering to vote (if eligible) in next year's state elections.
